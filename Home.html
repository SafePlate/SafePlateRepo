Dietary Preferences

SafePlate's Diet Preferences component is designed to cater to individuals with diverse dietary choices and requirements. Whether you're a vegetarian, vegan, pescatarian, or follow specific dietary guidelines like keto or paleo, this feature ensures that your meal options align with your preferences. Simply input your dietary choices, and SafePlate will curate personalized meal suggestions, making it effortless to maintain your diet plan while enjoying a variety of delicious and satisfying meals.

Allergy Checker

Safety is a top priority when it comes to food, especially for individuals with allergies. SafePlate's Allergy Checker component provides peace of mind by allowing users to scan food products or enter ingredients to check for potential allergens. Whether you're allergic to nuts, gluten, dairy, or any other common allergen, this feature quickly identifies products or recipes that might trigger your allergies. It helps you make informed choices, ensuring your meals are safe, delicious, and tailored to your specific dietary needs.

Calorie and Macro Calculator

Achieving your fitness goals is easier with SafePlate's Calorie and Macro Calculator. This component empowers users to track their daily calorie intake and monitor macronutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. Whether you're trying to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain a balanced diet, input your meals and recipes to instantly receive detailed nutritional information. SafePlate's intuitive interface makes it simple to keep track of your progress, helping you stay on target and make healthier food choices that align with your fitness objectives.
