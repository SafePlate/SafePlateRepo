Frequently Asked Questions

How does SafePlate make revenue?

To ensure a pleasant user experience, SafePlate is 100% free and makes most of its revenue from non-intrusive advertisements

What devices support the SafePlate app?

The SafePlate application is supported on IOS, Android, and Windows operating systems

Which restaurant chains have SafePlate support?

For a comprehensive list of which restaurants are partnered with safePlate in your local region visit the "restaurants" tab in the app

How do I know I can trust SafePlate with by personal well-being?

SafePlate has been rigorously and meticulously revised by Health Canada to ensure a safe user experience
